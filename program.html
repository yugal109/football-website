FITNESS / NUTRITION

We believe that fitness and nutritional plan plays a major role in training and it is vital for a player to maintain proper hydration during practice. A healthy and balanced diet can support a player to remain consistent throughout the game and every player should stay committed to their personal nutritional goals to achieve the target.

Fitness program will be a part of our weekly routine and nutritional advice will be given to our players to stay in shape physically and mentally.
